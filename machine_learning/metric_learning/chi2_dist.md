__Chi-square Distance__
--------    
---       
*    __Formula__         
$$
\hspace{8mm}\chi^2{(I,M)} = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{(I_j - M_j)^2}{I_i+M_j + eps}
$$       
<br />    
    
__Weighted Chi-square Distance__
--------    
---    
*    __Formula__         
$$
\hspace{8mm}\chi^2{(I,M)} = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{w_j(I_j - M_j)^2}{I_i+M_j + eps}\\
